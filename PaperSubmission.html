Paper Submission for SCOPES-2024 Conference


Submission Guidelines

SCOPES-2024 welcomes papers related to the conference tracks. For further information, please refer to the Call for Papers.

The following instructions should be followed to make a successful submission:


Paper Size:

Please ensure pinned manuscript adheres to the specified length requirement of 4-6 pages with Abstract word count limit 150-250 words, following the IEEE Conference format guidelines. You can download the template from the provided link:


Paper Template:

Click here for MS Word Template
Click here for LaTeX Template
IEEE - Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings

Note : Manuscripts that do not conform to the formatting guidelines will be removed from further consideration without review.


Paper Submission Method:

The online paper submission system opens from July 1st, 2024. Please read over the paper kit for formatting instructions. Papers should be submitted via Microsoft CMT Link

Instructions:

1. Upload papers in IEEE format only.

2. Remove the author details from the manuscript for double blind review.

3. Select the track as per suitable theme of the conference as mentioned in the Author Guidelines.


In case of any uploading issue, email us at scopes2024@cutm.ac.in


IEEE Policies:

Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission).


Plagiarism:

Learn how to avoid plagiarism. IEEE defines plagiarism as the use of another’s ideas, processes, results, or words without explicitly acknowledging the original author and source. Plagiarism in any form is unacceptable and is considered a serious breach of professional conduct, with potentially severe ethical and legal consequences ( IEEE Publication Services and Products Board Operations Manual, Section 8.2.1.B.7. ). Follow proper citation practices noted above to avoid plagiarism. All papers are checked for plagiarism before review process and the plagiarism should not exceed 20%.
