Title of the Talk:
Design of data driven modern electronics systems in the era of cloud and AI


Abstract

In the session, we will cover how hyper scalers like Azure and artificial intelligence offerings from OpenAI make complex electronics and communications system design secure, collaborative, cost efficient and scalable. We will discuss how data integration and consumption play a key role. We will cover how cloud and AI together make modern systems developed with examples from security, healthcare industries. Modern signal processing happens more on the edge cloud at first. We will discuss how edge and central cloud pave away for smarter signal processing systems of future.
