SAFWAN GHANEM

Software Engineer at Barclays in Prague, Czechia

Purna brings in 28 years of experience and expertise in engineering, sales, business development, marketing and operations, and connecting these specialties to build a successful business. His love for Silicon Valley and its technology vibe accompanied by a passion for propagating Silicon Valley’s innovative culture around the world drove him to start MarqueeSemi and SignatureIP with a R&D presence in the 'best kept secret' locations around the world. Prior to founding MarqueeSemi and SignatureIP, Purna held the position of vice president engineering and operation at Tessolve USA. He brings in entrepreneurial vision to his companies – with his experience in co-founding or leading successful start-ups – ASIC Architect, Aims Technology, Kool Chip, Platys Communication, SmartSAN Network. Also, he had provided engineering and operational leadership at established companies – Adaptec, Semtech, Broadcom, NXP and Tessolve. He holds 5 US patents in the area of SoC verification and design. Purna holds a BSEE from NIT Rourkela, India and MSEE from University of Toledo, OH, USA.


Title of the Talk:
The Art of Custom Silicon Design – India’s need of the hour.


Abstract

The keynote discusses the future of silicon design, transitioning from monolithic silicon systems to chiplet-based silicon systems.

It will explore the art of mastering this transition, key technologies involved, and current challenges in the industry. The growing market for chiplet-based systems presents significant opportunities worldwide.

The keynote will elaborate on how India can be positioned to take advantage of this opportunity with a cohesive ecosystem of fabless semiconductor startups, universities, and government support.

It will highlight the critical role of the government in promoting a vibrant, custom silicon design ecosystem.
