Mrs. Renuka Shrinivasan

Sales Director for Dassault Systems

Renuka SRINIVASAN is currently 3DEXPERIENCE Edu Sales Director for Dassault Systems India. In her current role, she is responsible for promoting academic transformation with experience based learning in India. The aim to create a workforce of the future that is Industry ready. Prior to this, she was Brand Sales Director, SIMULIA for Dassault Systems India responsible for the simulation business in India. Renuka has over twenty five years of experience in the simulation (or computer-aided engineering) business and was instrumental in setting up the Abaqus business in India. She was responsible for the pre-sales and post-sales technical activities as well as business development for India. She has worked with customers across a variety of industries including automotive, aerospace and defense, industrial equipment manufacturers, civil engineering, engineering services providers, among others. Renuka has a Bachelors in Civil Engineering from BITS, Pilani, and a Masters from Duke University. She joined Hibbitt, Karlsson and Sorensen Inc, and worked with them in USA before returning to India to set up Abaqus India.
