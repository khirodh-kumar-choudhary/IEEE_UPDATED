Speakers

Prof. Naeem Mannoon

University Of Teknologi MARA, Malaysia

Title of the Talk: 'Advanced Automation of Substations-lates trends'

Obtained his B.Sc. with Distinction In Electrical and Electronic Engineering, M.Sc. Electrical Energy, and PhD in Power System.
Dr. Naeem is an active researcher and consultant, has extensive hands-on experience gained from four years working experience at Kuwait Thermal Power Plant and two years at EPE Reyrolle where he designed and commissioned power substation . Dr. Naeem has provided numerous consultation and technical training services to various countries in Asia and Europe. In addition, Dr Naeem has represented University Technology Mara (UiTM) as an invited Keynote speaker in several countries such as US, Turkey, Japan and India. Dr. Naeem has also conducted many doctoral examinations for local and international students. He is a technical reviewer for prestigious journals and has successfully completed 2 government research funds and several research funds at university level. His expertise includes Soft computing, Optimization Techniques, UPFC, renewable energy, Power Stability and protection.

Prof. Paolo Rocca

Organizations (WFEO).

Title of the Talk: 'Building' Smart EM Environments for Future Wireless Communication Systems Through AI.

Biography: Paolo Rocca (IEEE Fellow 2023) received the MS degree in Telecommunications Engineering (summa cum laude) in 2005 and the Ph.D. Degree in Information and Communication Technologies in 2008 from the University of Trento, Italy. He is currently Associate Professor at the Department of Civil, Environmental, and Mechanical Engineering (University of Trento), Huashan Scholar Chair Professor at the Xidian University, Xi'an, China, and a member of the ELEDIA Research Center. Moreover, he is Member of the 6G Data and AI Working Group for the Committee on Engineering for Innovative Technologies (CEIT) of the World Federation of Engineering Organizations (WFEO). Prof. Rocca received the National Scientific Qualification for the position of Full Professor in Italy and France in April 2017 and January 2020, respectively.
