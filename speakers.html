Prof. (Dr.) Naeem Hannoon

Prof. (Dr.)Naeem Hannoon

University Of Teknologi MARA, Malaysia

Obtained his B.Sc. with Distinction In Electrical and Electronic Engineering, M.Sc. Electrical Energy, and PhD in Power System. Prof. (Dr.)Naeem is an active researcher and consultant, has extensive hands-on experience gained from four years working experience at Kuwait Thermal Power Plant and two years at EPE Reyrolle where he designed and commissioned power substation . Dr. Naeem has provided numerous consultation and technical training services to various countries in Asia and Europe. In addition, Dr Naeem has represented University Technology Mara (UiTM) as an invited Keynote speaker in several countries such as US, Turkey, Japan and India. Dr. Naeem has also conducted many doctoral examinations for local and international students. He is a technical reviewer for prestigious journals and has successfully completed 2 government research funds and several research funds at university level. His expertise includes Soft computing, Optimization Techniques, UPFC, renewable energy, Power Stability and protection.


Title of the Talk:
Advanced Automation of Substations-lates trends


Abstract

A substation automation system is a collection of hardware and software components that are used to monitor and control an electrical system, both locally and remotely. In this presentation, Smart Grid is introduced that is capable to self-healing, equipped with dynamic optimization techniques, using real-time measuring equipment to minimize network losses, maintain voltage system and frequency, increase reliability system and optimize assets management, therefore, these digital substations outperform conventional ones in improving overall operations and maintenance. It deploys HMI (human machine interference) at each substation and with aid of IEC 61850 protocols; it communicate intelligently with other devices through Generic Object-Oriented Substation Event (GOOSE) messaging promoting supreme control and monitoring capabilities. vendor interoperability and intelligent communication.
